4275 Frontal Face Images annotated with boundary box positions and 68 landmark key points

This dataset is made based on images and annotations related to Train dataset of wider face image. There are 61 directories inside of train folder which are pruned, cleansed, and reannotated. The new annotation is a mixing of the previous annotation (x y w h) and the landmark key points listed right back-to-back each other delimited by space character: 68 (x y) points
